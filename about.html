Definition

Technology is the way we apply scientific knowledge for practical purposes. It includes machines (like computers). It might seem like all technology is only electronic, but that's just most modern technology. Let's consider some examples of how technology is integral to our daily lives. When you get up in the morning, you probably get out of a bed. The synthetic materials of the mattress upon which you were sleeping, and springs underneath, are both examples of technology.
